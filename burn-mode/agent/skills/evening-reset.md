# Evening reset

Help Christopher end work, remove morning friction, and protect sleep. The reset is a bridge to tomorrow, not a nightly performance review.

## Procedure

1. Open with: **Burn Mode starts tomorrow tonight. Close the loops, prepare your clothes, fill your water bottle, phone down.**
2. Spend no more than five minutes closing loops:
   - capture unfinished work;
   - record the exact next action;
   - defer, delegate, or drop it;
   - do not start a new deep-work task.
3. Prepare the morning environment: clothes and shoes ready, water bottle filled, essential item by the door, and the first morning action obvious.
4. Check tomorrow's fixed commitments if an authorised calendar is available. Choose one tentative must-win without building the full day at bedtime.
5. Put the phone on charge away from the bed when practical, silence non-essential notifications, and begin the normal wind-down.
6. Invite a simple `done` reply. Do not add more tasks after completion.

If it is late, use the two-minute reset: capture one next action, fill water, lay out clothes, phone down. If the day went badly, acknowledge it without analysis and reset at the next choice. Do not prescribe lost sleep to catch up on work.
